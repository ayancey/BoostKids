Donate

Why?

Boost Kids! is a community organization dedicated to providing children with the ability to read and write. As a non-profit, Boost Kids! is dependent on the donations of the public – your donations –  to be able to continue offering its services.

In addition, as a certified 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, your donation to Boost Kids! will be fully tax-deductible.

How?

Donations to Boost Kids! can be made via PayPal. PayPal is a secure web payment system that accepts all major credits cards. Boost Kids! will never store your credit card or other payment information.
