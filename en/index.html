Welcome to Boost Kids!

Boost Kids! is an academic literacy-centered program to give kids in San Bruno public schools and surrounding areas a “boost” to support literacy skills. Boost Kids! will focus on the primary areas of reading, phoneme segmentation, phonics, vocabulary, fluency, and comprehension.

Dates: Summer 2015

The program will take place during the summer months for four weeks:

Monday, June 22nd – Friday, July 17th from 8:45 AM to 12:30 PM

as well as throughout the school year, after the school day, over four-week intervals with two-week breaks at the holidays and based on the school calendars.

Monday – Friday from 3:15 PM to 5:30 PM
